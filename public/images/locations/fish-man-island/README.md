# Fish-Man Island Location Images

This directory contains location images for Fish-Man Island - the underwater kingdom 10,000 meters below the Red Line.

   - Importance: Royal residence, Shirahoshi's prison tower, center of kingdom

## Image Guidelines

### Style
- Underwater environment with bubble barriers
- Bioluminescent coral and sea life
- Mix of vibrant beauty and dark tragedy
- Bubble coating effects around structures
- Glowing atmospheric lighting

### Composition
- Emphasize 10,000 meters depth atmosphere
- Show bubble barriers protecting areas
- Coral architecture and organic structures
- Contrast between palace beauty and district poverty
- Ancient history visible in Sea Forest

### Quality
- High resolution (minimum 1920x1080px recommended)
- Clear underwater lighting effects
- Bioluminescent glow visible
- Minimal compression artifacts
- Consistent art style across locations

### Content Focus
- Underwater kingdom atmosphere
- Organic coral-based architecture
- Cultural locations (palace, cafe, dojo)
- Historical sites (Sea Forest, Noah's Ark)
- Contrast between hope and despair
