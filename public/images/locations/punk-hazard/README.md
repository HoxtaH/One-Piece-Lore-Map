# Punk Hazard Location Images

This directory contains location images for Punk Hazard - the island of fire and ice permanently changed by the Akainu vs Aokiji duel.

## Required Images (6 Total)

1. **burning-lands.jpg** - Burning Lands (Fire Side)
   - Half of island engulfed in eternal flames from Akainu's magma
   - Key features: Rivers of lava, constant fire, scorched wasteland, fire-breathing dragons
   - Atmosphere: Scorching heat, volcanic, dangerous, Akainu's power residue
   - Importance: Shows Admiral duel aftermath, extreme fire environment

2. **ice-lands.jpg** - Ice Lands (Frozen Side)
   - Half of island frozen in perpetual ice from Aokiji's power
   - Key features: Eternal snow, ice formations, blizzards, frozen landscape
   - Atmosphere: Freezing cold, arctic wasteland, Aokiji's power residue
   - Importance: Entry point for Straw Hats, shows duel aftermath

3. **laboratory.jpg** - Central Laboratory
   - Massive research facility where Caesar conducts experiments
   - Key features: Multi-floor complex, monitoring rooms, climate-controlled, Vegapunk's old equipment
   - Atmosphere: Scientific, sinister, mad scientist lair
   - Importance: Main location, later cut in half by Law

4. **sad-room.jpg** - SAD Production Room
   - Secret manufacturing facility for SMILE ingredient
   - Key features: Chemical equipment, production machinery, heavily secured
   - Atmosphere: Industrial, secretive, critical to plot
   - Importance: Target of Law's plan, key to Kaido's army

5. **biscuits-room.jpg** - Biscuits Room
   - Holding area for giant children
   - Key features: Large room for giant children, candy distribution area, tragic conditions
   - Atmosphere: Sad, manipulative, victim holding area
   - Importance: Most emotionally powerful location, children's rescue

6. **central-lake.jpg** - Central Lake
   - Border where fire and ice meet in the middle
   - Key features: Steam rising, fire on one side ice on other, perfect division
   - Atmosphere: Neutral zone, visual spectacle, symbolic
   - Importance: Shows exact point of Akainu/Aokiji clash

## Image Guidelines

### Style
- Extreme split between fire and ice aesthetics
- Scientific research facility elements
- Post-apocalyptic abandoned government base
- Contrast between natural extremes and artificial lab
- Dark tone reflecting child experimentation horror

### Composition
- Emphasize fire/ice split dramatically
- Show scale of island transformation
- Laboratory should feel sinister and clinical
- Central lake as perfect fire-ice divide
- Wasteland feeling from Admiral duel

### Quality
- High resolution (minimum 1920x1080px recommended)
- Clear fire and ice effects visible
- Laboratory interior details
- Minimal compression artifacts
- Consistent art style across locations

### Content Focus
- Visual impact of Admiral duel on environment
- Mad scientist laboratory atmosphere
- Contrast between natural disasters and human evil
- Children's imprisonment conditions
- Strategic importance of locations

### Atmosphere Notes
- **Burning Lands**: Hellish, volcanic, constantly on fire, unbearable heat
- **Ice Lands**: Arctic, frozen wasteland, blizzards, bitter cold
- **Laboratory**: Clinical yet sinister, mad science, Vegapunk's legacy corrupted
- **SAD Room**: Industrial, secretive, heavily guarded, plot-critical
- **Biscuits Room**: Tragic, manipulative, children held here, emotional rescue site
- **Central Lake**: Neutral zone, fire meets ice, symbolic division, steam rising

### Recommended Sources
1. Official One Piece manga panels from Punk Hazard Arc (Chapters 654-699)
2. One Piece anime screenshots from Episodes 579-625
3. Official location art and backgrounds
4. High-quality renders showing fire/ice split
5. Fan art capturing extreme environments (with proper attribution)

### Special Considerations
- Fire and ice should look eternal, not temporary
- Laboratory was cut in half by Law - can show before or after
- Central lake steam should be prominent visual
- Biscuits room should convey tragedy of children's situation
- SAD room importance to plot should be clear
- Show Vegapunk's original work vs Caesar's corruption

### Key Moments by Location
- **Burning Lands**: Straw Hats encounter fire dragons, extreme heat survival
- **Ice Lands**: Entry point, frozen landscape exploration, meeting samurai parts
- **Laboratory**: Caesar's broadcasts, children discovery, main battles, cut in half
- **SAD Room**: Discovery of SMILE production, Law's target, plan revealed
- **Biscuits Room**: Giant children crying for candy, rescue mission, Chopper's work
- **Central Lake**: Visual representation of Akainu vs Aokiji duel result

### Emotional Themes
- Consequences of Admiral duel (permanent island change)
- Mad science without morality (laboratory)
- Child suffering and rescue (biscuits room)
- Underworld manufacturing (SAD room)
- Nature vs science (fire/ice vs laboratory)
- Abandoned government facility turned evil

### Technical Details
- Fire side: Lava rivers, constant flames, volcanic rocks
- Ice side: Thick snow, ice formations, blizzards
- Laboratory: Multi-floor, monitoring systems, research equipment
- Perfect division at central lake
- Steam effects where temperatures clash
- Climate control in lab versus extremes outside
