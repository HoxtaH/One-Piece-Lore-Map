## Image Specifications
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 1200x800px minimum
- Aspect ratio: 3:2 or 16:9 for landscape shots
- Quality: High resolution to capture winter scenery

## Visual Themes
- **Color Palette**: White (snow), pink (cherry blossoms), blue (sky/ice)
- **Mood**: Cold but hopeful, desolate yet beautiful
- **Key Elements**: Snow, mountains, castle, cherry blossom petals, Jolly Roger
