# Alabasta Kingdom Location Images

This directory contains location images for the Alabasta Kingdom sub-locations.

## Required Images

- `alubarna.jpg` - Capital city with royal palace and clock tower
- `rainbase.jpg` - Casino city with Rain Dinners (Crocodile's base)
- `nanohana.jpg` - Port city famous for perfume production
- `yuba.jpg` - Dried oasis and rebel base (Toto's well)
- `katorea.jpg` - City on Sandora River (battle site)
- `sandora-desert.jpg` - Vast desert with sand dunes and ruins
- `sandora-river.jpg` - Great river lifeline of the kingdom
- `royal-tomb.jpg` - Underground tomb with Poneglyph (Luffy vs Crocodile)

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG
- **Dimensions**: 800x600px or 1200x800px recommended
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 3:2 preferred
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for web performance

## Notes

- Desert/sandy color schemes throughout
- Arabian/Middle Eastern architectural influences
- Show scale and vastness of the kingdom
- Alubarna palace should look royal and grand
- Yuba should show drought devastation
- Royal tomb should feel ancient and mysterious
- Sandora Desert needs to convey danger and heat
