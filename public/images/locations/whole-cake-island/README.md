## Story Context

Whole Cake Island is the central island of Totto Land, Big Mom's 35-island archipelago where everything is made of food. The entire landscape is brought to life by Big Mom's Soul-Soul Fruit, creating homies (living objects) that sing, dance, and spy on everyone. The seemingly whimsical fairy tale kingdom masks a dark reality of fear, tribute (lifespan payments), and Big Mom's overwhelming power.

The Whole Cake Chateau is where the ill-fated wedding between Sanji and Pudding took place. What was supposed to be a political marriage became the site of an assassination attempt, wedding disruption, and Big Mom's rampage after Luffy destroyed her Mother Carmel photo. The Tamatebako treasure box (from Fish-Man Island) exploded at the base, causing the entire chateau to collapse.

The Mirror World, created by Brûlée's Mirror-Mirror Fruit, became crucial to both infiltration and escape. It's where the legendary 12-hour battle between Luffy and Katakuri occurred - one of the greatest fights in One Piece history. The mirror dimension connects all mirrors in Totto Land, providing instant travel.

The Seducing Woods captured Sanji's group initially, with aggressive tree and animal homies attacking anyone who enters. Pudding initially 'rescued' them as part of her deception.

Cacao Island's harbor was the final battleground where Luffy emerged victorious from the Mirror World, where Sanji baked the legendary wedding cake to stop Big Mom's rampage, and where the desperate escape culminated. Pedro sacrificed himself on the coast to damage Perospero and give the crew a chance to escape.
