# Marineford Location Images

This directory contains location images for Marineford - the former Marine Headquarters and site of the Paramount War.

## Required Images (6 Total)

1. **execution-platform.jpg** - The Execution Platform
   - The raised stage where Ace was to be executed
   - Key features: Platform, executioners, shackles, broadcast cameras
   - Atmosphere: Tense, symbolic center of the war
   - Importance: Where Ace was freed and where he died nearby

2. **bay.jpg** - Marineford Bay
   - The frozen bay where Whitebeard's fleet emerged
   - Key features: Frozen tsunami, ice battlefield, Moby Dick emergence
   - Atmosphere: Massive scale, frozen warfare
   - Importance: Initial battles, Whitebeard's dramatic entrance

3. **plaza.jpg** - Main Plaza
   - Central battlefield where major fights occurred
   - Key features: Admirals vs Commanders, mass combat, Luffy's charge
   - Atmosphere: Chaotic warfare, clashing powers
   - Importance: Where most of the war's action happened

4. **fortress.jpg** - Marine Fortress
   - The massive headquarters building
   - Key features: Marine symbol, command center, battle damage
   - Atmosphere: Military might, government power
   - Importance: Symbol of Marine authority, heavily damaged in war

5. **oris-plaza.jpg** - Oris Plaza
   - Where Whitebeard made his final stand
   - Key features: Whitebeard's death spot, One Piece declaration
   - Atmosphere: Legendary, end of an era
   - Importance: Most iconic location - Whitebeard died standing here

6. **waters.jpg** - Surrounding Waters
   - The Calm Belt waters around Marineford
   - Key features: Battleship blockade, Gates of Justice visible
   - Atmosphere: Naval might, strategic positioning
   - Importance: Where fleets gathered and where Law escaped with Luffy

## Image Guidelines

### Style
- Epic scale befitting the greatest war
- Military fortress architecture
- Massive destruction and battle damage
- Mix of order (Marine) and chaos (war)
- Tragic and legendary atmosphere

### Composition
- Wide shots showing scale of war
- Frozen bay and tsunami emphasizing power
- Marine symbols and military precision
- Contrast between order and destruction
- Legendary moments captured in locations

### Quality
- High resolution (minimum 1920x1080px recommended)
- Clear architectural details
- Dramatic lighting for war atmosphere
- Minimal compression artifacts
- Consistent art style across locations

### Content Focus
- The scale of the Paramount War
- Marine Headquarters as seat of power
- Destruction from greatest battles
- Legendary moments tied to specific locations
- The cost and tragedy of war

### Atmosphere Notes
- **Execution Platform**: Tense, symbolic, center of attention
- **Bay**: Epic entrance, frozen battlefield, tsunami power
- **Plaza**: Chaos, clashing powers, main warfare
- **Fortress**: Government might, heavily damaged, command center
- **Oris Plaza**: Legendary, Whitebeard's last stand, era ending
- **Waters**: Naval blockade, escape route, strategic importance

### Recommended Sources
1. Official One Piece manga panels from Marineford Arc (Chapters 550-580)
2. One Piece anime screenshots from Episodes 457-489
3. Official location art and backgrounds
4. High-quality renders of Marine Headquarters
5. Fan art capturing the war's scale (with proper attribution)

### Special Considerations
- Show the fortress before and after damage
- Frozen bay is iconic to the war
- Execution platform is symbolic center
- Oris Plaza is where Whitebeard declared One Piece is real
- Scale should feel overwhelming and epic
- Mix pristine Marine order with war destruction

### Key Moments by Location
- **Execution Platform**: Ace's shackles, brief freedom, death nearby
- **Bay**: Whitebeard emerging, tsunami frozen, initial clashes
- **Plaza**: Luffy's charge, Admiral fights, Conqueror's Haki
- **Fortress**: Sengoku commanding, broadcast room, Marine symbol
- **Oris Plaza**: Whitebeard's final stand, One Piece declaration, death standing
