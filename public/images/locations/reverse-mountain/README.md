## Image Specifications
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 800x600px minimum
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3 preferred
- Quality: High resolution landscape images

## Naming Convention
Use lowercase with hyphens:
- ✅ `twin-capes.jpg`
- ✅ `inside-laboon.jpg`
