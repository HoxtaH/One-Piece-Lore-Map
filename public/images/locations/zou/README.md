## Story Context

Zou is located on the back of Zunesha, a colossal elephant that has been walking through the ocean for over 1,000 years as punishment for an ancient crime. The island is called the "Phantom Island" because it's constantly moving and nearly impossible to find without a Vivre Card. 

Seventeen days before the Straw Hats arrived, Jack the Drought attacked Zou searching for the ninja Raizo. When the Minks refused to betray their ally, Jack unleashed Caesar's poison gas, decimating the population and destroying much of the kingdom. Inuarashi lost a leg, Nekomamushi lost an arm, and countless Minks died. Yet not a single one betrayed Raizo.

The Whale Tree contains one of the four Road Poneglyphs necessary to reach Laugh Tale, entrusted to the Minks by the Kozuki Clan. This sacred bond led to the formation of the Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance to liberate Wano and take down Kaido.
