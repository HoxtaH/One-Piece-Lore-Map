## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG preferred
- **Aspect Ratio**: Portrait orientation (3:4 or similar)
- **Resolution**: Minimum 800x1000px recommended
- **Quality**: High-quality anime screenshots or official art
- **Naming**: Lowercase, hyphenated (e.g., `inuarashi.jpg`)

## Emotional Themes

- **Loyalty**: Minks protected Raizo at cost of kingdom
- **Sacrifice**: Inuarashi's leg, Nekomamushi's arm, Pedro's lifespan
- **Feud**: Inuarashi and Nekomamushi can't see each other (reconcile later)
- **Warrior Pride**: Every Mink is born warrior
- **Ancient Bonds**: Connection to Kozuki Clan and Oden
- **Brutality**: Jack's merciless poison gas attack
- **Recovery**: Healing after near-genocide
- **Alliance**: Unity despite differences to face Kaido

## Special Considerations

- **Electro**: Many images should show electric crackling (signature Mink ability)
- **Sulong**: Some characters have powerful full moon transformation (white fur, red eyes, immense power)
- **Damage**: Post-Jack attack images may show injuries, bandages, recovery
- **Day/Night**: Inuarashi (day) and Nekomamushi (night) rule separately
- **Animal Features**: Each Mink has distinct animal characteristics (ears, tail, fur patterns)
- **Zou's Destruction**: Background may show damaged Mokomo Dukedom
- **Raizo Reveal**: Emotional moment when hidden ninja shown
- **Jack's Return**: Zunesha destroys fleet with trunk

## Story Context

The Zou arc follows the Straw Hats arriving at the moving island to discover Jack's brutal poison gas attack 17 days prior. The Minks endured genocide rather than betray Raizo, proving loyalty beyond measure. Inuarashi and Nekomamushi, despite their feud, both love their people and form the Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance with Luffy. The revelation of Raizo being safe all along is one of One Piece's most emotional moments. Sanji's wedding announcement splits the crew, with the Sanji Retrieval Team heading to Whole Cake Island.
