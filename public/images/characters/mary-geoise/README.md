# Mary Geoise Character Images

This directory contains character images for Mary Geoise (The Holy Land), the capital of the World Government atop the Red Line.

## Required Images (18 characters)

### Supreme Rulers

**im.jpg** - Im (Secret Sovereign)
- The mysterious supreme ruler who secretly sits on the Empty Throne
- Appearance: Tall, slender figure with crown and robes, face often in shadow
- KEY VISUAL: Eyes with strange circular patterns (Rinnegan-like appearance)
- Gender ambiguous - androgynous features
- Carrying a butterfly or having butterflies nearby (symbolism)
- Cold, otherworldly presence
- Sometimes shown cutting wanted posters with a blade
- Crown and royal regalia
- Scene options: Sitting on Empty Throne, in freezer room with giant straw hat, cutting posters of Luffy/Blackbeard/Vivi/Shirahoshi
- Aura of absolute power and ancient mystery

## Image Requirements

### Technical Specifications
- Format: JPG
- Recommended size: 400x600px minimum
- Aspect ratio: 2:3 (portrait orientation)
- Quality: High resolution for character details

### Style Guidelines
- Anime/manga style preferred (One Piece aesthetic)
- Clear view of character's distinctive features
- Appropriate background context (Mary Geoise architecture, Empty Throne, etc.)
- Character tags should be visible where applicable (bubble helmets, brands, transformations)

### Special Considerations

**Im:**
- Most mysterious character - very few clear appearances
- Shadow and mystery are key elements
- Crown and strange eyes are main identifiers
- Gender ambiguous presentation important
- Butterfly symbolism
- Scene with giant frozen straw hat is iconic

**Five Elders:**
- ALL wear black formal suits
- Can show human form OR yokai demon transformations
- Each has distinct hairstyle/feature for identification
- Ancient, powerful, but in elderly bodies
- Immortal beings - can show wounds regenerating

**Bubble Helmets:**
- CRITICAL for Celestial Dragons (except reformed Mjosgard)
- Glass bubble filled with "pure air"
- Symbol of their supremacy complex
- Should be clearly visible

**Slave Brands:**
- "Hoof of the Soaring Dragon" mark
- Burned into skin (chest or back usually)
- Fisher Tiger and Kuma both have this (Fisher Tiger covered with Sun Pirates tattoo)
- Painful, degrading symbol
- Don't show gratuitously but historically accurate to story

**Size Differences:**
- Shirahoshi is ENORMOUS (11+ meters) - needs to be shown much larger than humans
- Kuma is very large (~7 meters)
- Normal humans appear tiny next to them

**Emotional Themes:**
- Supreme Power: Im and Five Elders radiate ancient authority
- Oppression: Celestial Dragons' cruelty, slaves' suffering
- Heroism: Mjosgard's redemption, Fisher Tiger's liberation, Sabo's courage, Cobra's sacrifice
- Mystery: Shanks' true allegiance, Im's identity, Vivi's disappearance
- Tragedy: Cobra's murder, Kuma's enslavement, Fisher Tiger's death
- Revolution: Sabo and Revolutionary Army's defiance, Fisher Tiger's raid

## Character Relationships

**Power Hierarchy:**
- Im (secret supreme ruler) → Five Elders (public leaders) → Celestial Dragons (nobility) → Everyone else (beneath them)

**Allies:**
- Revolutionary Army: Sabo, Kuma (rescued), Dragon (off-screen)
- Martyrs: Cobra, Mjosgard, Fisher Tiger
- Victims seeking justice: Vivi, Shirahoshi

**Enemies:**
- Im and Five Elders (absolute evil)
- Cruel Celestial Dragons: Charlos, Rosward
- Oppressive system

**Enigmas:**
- Shanks (meeting with Elders - why?)
- Im's true identity and motives
- Vivi's disappearance

## Notes

Mary Geoise represents the ultimate corruption and inequality in One Piece. Images should reflect:
- **Opulence vs. Suffering:** Lavish wealth built on slavery
- **False Democracy:** Empty Throne symbolism vs. Im's secret rule
- **Absolute Power:** Im and Five Elders' ancient authority
- **Heroic Resistance:** Fisher Tiger's legendary raid, Sabo's infiltration, Mjosgard's redemption
- **Tragedy:** Cobra's murder, Kuma's enslavement, the fate of slaves
- **Mystery:** Giant straw hat, Im's identity, Shanks' meeting, Void Century secrets

The visual contrast between the pristine white architecture and golden wealth of Mary Geoise versus the brutal reality of slavery and murder should be evident.

This is the political and moral center of the One Piece world's corruption - the place where the biggest lies are told and the greatest evils are committed under the guise of divine rule.
