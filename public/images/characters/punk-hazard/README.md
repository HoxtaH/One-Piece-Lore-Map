## Image Guidelines

### Style
- Split fire/ice atmosphere reflecting island
- Scientific/laboratory elements for Caesar's facility
- Post-timeskip Straw Hat designs
- Serious tone with some comic elements
- Mad science and experimentation horror

### Quality
- High resolution (minimum 800x800px recommended)
- Clear character features and Devil Fruit powers
- Minimal compression artifacts
- Consistent art style preferred

### Naming Convention
- Use lowercase with hyphens for multi-word names
- Format: `character-name.jpg`
- Examples: `caesar.jpg`, `kinemon.jpg`, `baby5.jpg`

### Content Focus
- Devil Fruit powers prominently displayed
- Character's role in arc (scientist, victim, ally, enemy)
- Key transformations (Monet's harpy, Momonosuke's dragon)
- Emotional themes (children's suffering, alliance formation)

### Recommended Sources
1. Official One Piece manga panels from Punk Hazard Arc (Chapters 654-699)
2. One Piece anime screenshots from Episodes 579-625
3. Official character art from Punk Hazard period
4. High-quality fan art (with proper attribution)

### Special Considerations
- **Caesar**: Show gas body and mad scientist personality
- **Monet**: Harpy form with wing arms and talon legs clearly visible
- **Vergo**: Food on face is signature trait, full-body Haki
- **Law**: Strategic cool demeanor, alliance proposer
- **Kin'emon**: Traditional samurai introduction to Wano
- **Momonosuke**: Pink dragon form is key visual
- **Children**: Show their victimhood and giant size
- **Smiley**: Always smiling despite being deadly poison

### Emotional Themes
- Mad science without ethics (Caesar)
- Child exploitation and rescue
- Unlikely alliances forming (Straw Hats + Law + Marines)
- Betrayal and undercover operations (Vergo, Monet)
- Introduction to larger conflicts (Wano, Kaido)
- Fire and ice extremes

### Devil Fruit Showcase
- **Caesar**: Gas-Gas Fruit (purple gas, oxygen removal)
- **Monet**: Snow-Snow Fruit (snow attacks, harpy form)
- **Law**: Ope-Ope Fruit (Room, body swapping, cutting)
- **Smoker**: Smoke-Smoke Fruit (white smoke, jitte)
- **Kin'emon**: Fuku-Fuku Fruit (cloth creation)
- **Momonosuke**: Artificial Dragon Fruit (pink dragon)
- **Baby 5**: Arms-Arms Fruit (weapon transformations)
- **Buffalo**: Spin-Spin Fruit (propeller flight)
