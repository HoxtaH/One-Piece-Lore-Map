## Story Context

The Whole Cake Island arc is the Sanji Retrieval Mission where Luffy's team infiltrated Big Mom's territory to bring back their cook. Sanji's tragic past with the abusive Vinsmoke Family was revealed - his father Judge genetically modified his children to be emotionless super-soldiers, but Sanji 'failed' and retained his humanity. The arranged marriage to Pudding was a trap to massacre the Vinsmokes and steal Germa technology.

The wedding descended into chaos when Luffy destroyed Mother Carmel's photo (Big Mom's greatest treasure), shattering her psyche. Bege's assassination attempt failed, and the Straw Hats barely escaped. The legendary 12-hour battle between Luffy and Katakuri in the Mirror World became one of the greatest fights in One Piece, with both warriors gaining mutual respect.

Pedro sacrificed himself to damage Perospero and allow the crew's escape. Sanji baked a legendary wedding cake with Pudding and Chiffon that stopped Big Mom's rampage by being so delicious it knocked her unconscious. The arc ended with the Straw Hats escaping Big Mom's territory alive, and Luffy being declared the 'Fifth Emperor' by the news.
