## Image Guidelines

### Style
- Underwater atmosphere with bioluminescent lighting
- Mix of beauty (palace/mermaids) and darkness (hatred/discrimination)
- Vibrant coral colors
- Emotional depth for tragic backstories

### Quality
- High resolution (minimum 800x800px recommended)
- Clear character features (fish/mermaid traits visible)
- Minimal compression artifacts
- Consistent art style preferred

### Naming Convention
- Use lowercase with hyphens for multi-word names
- Format: `character-name.jpg`
- Examples: `fisher-tiger.jpg`, `hody-jones.jpg`, `vander-decken.jpg`

### Content Focus
- Fish-man/mermaid characteristics prominently displayed
- Emotional themes: peace vs hatred, discrimination, hope
- Powers and abilities visible (Poseidon, fish-man karate, Devil Fruits)
- Historical weight (Fisher Tiger, Otohime as martyrs)

### Recommended Sources
1. Official One Piece manga panels from Fish-Man Island Arc (Chapters 603-653)
2. One Piece anime screenshots from Episodes 523-574
3. Official character art from Fish-Man Island period
4. High-quality fan art (with proper attribution)

### Special Considerations
- **Shirahoshi**: Show her giant size, crybaby nature, but also Ancient Weapon power
- **Otohime**: Fragile but determined, peaceful martyr
- **Fisher Tiger**: Heroic liberator with tragic end, sun symbol covering slave brand
- **Hody Jones**: Show Energy Steroid aging effects and empty inherited hatred
- **Jinbe**: Bridge between Straw Hats and Fish-Man Island, honorable warrior
- **Neptune Family**: Royal bearing with fish characteristics
- **Vander Decken**: Creepy stalker with obsession, gloved hands

### Emotional Themes
- Discrimination and inherited hatred
- Peace and coexistence dreams
- Tragedy of Queen Otohime and Fisher Tiger
- Ancient promises (Joyboy)
- Hope for future generations
- Family bonds (Neptune's love for children)

### Fish-Man/Mermaid Design Notes
- **Mermaids**: Upper body human, lower body fish tail, graceful
- **Fish-Men**: Humanoid with fish characteristics, can breathe air and water
- **Species variety**: Each character's fish type should be clear
- **Underwater adaptation**: Gills, fins, scales visible
- **Size differences**: Shirahoshi is giant, Otohime is tiny

## Notes
- This arc deals with heavy themes of racism and discrimination
- Focus on both the beauty of the kingdom and pain of history
- Characters represent different responses to discrimination
- Ancient Weapon Poseidon is central to world lore
- Fisher Tiger and Otohime are legendary tragic figures
- New generation (Shirahoshi, princes) carry hope for future
