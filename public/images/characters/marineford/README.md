## Image Guidelines

### Style
- Epic war atmosphere with high stakes
- Mix of heroic and tragic moments
- Strong emotions: grief, determination, rage
- Legendary scale befitting greatest war

### Quality
- High resolution (minimum 800x800px recommended)
- Clear character features and powers visible
- Minimal compression artifacts
- Consistent art style preferred

### Naming Convention
- Use lowercase with hyphens for multi-word names
- Format: `character-name.jpg`
- Examples: `whitebeard.jpg`, `akainu.jpg`, `marco.jpg`

### Content Focus
- Character's role in the war (deaths, key fights, moments)
- Devil Fruit powers prominently displayed
- Emotional weight of the Paramount War
- Legendary status and impact on One Piece world

### Recommended Sources
1. Official One Piece manga panels from Marineford Arc (Chapters 550-580)
2. One Piece anime screenshots from Episodes 457-489
3. Official character art from Marineford period
4. High-quality fan art capturing war moments (with proper attribution)

### Special Considerations
- **Whitebeard**: Show his power and final stand - died standing
- **Ace**: Fire powers and tragic death protecting Luffy
- **Akainu**: Ruthless magma power that killed Ace
- **Luffy**: Desperate rescue attempt and breakdown
- **Blackbeard**: Two Devil Fruit powers (unprecedented)
- **Shanks**: Commanding presence that stopped everyone
- **Garp**: Torn between duty and family (Luffy's grandfather)
- **Marco**: Phoenix regeneration and leadership
- **Sengoku**: Buddha form and strategic command

### Emotional Themes
- The weight of legends dying (Whitebeard, Ace)
- Family vs duty conflicts (Garp, Luffy)
- Absolute Justice vs moral justice
- The cost of war and sacrifice
- Era ending and new era beginning
- Brothers' bond (Luffy and Ace)

## Notes
- This arc features the most devastating losses in One Piece
- Focus on capturing both power and emotion
- The war changed the entire world of One Piece
- These characters define the Summit War Saga's climax
- Whitebeard's death and One Piece declaration are legendary
- Ace's death is the most heartbreaking moment in the series
